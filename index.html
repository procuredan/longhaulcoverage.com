Common questions

Can new trucking ventures get quotes?

Yes, but new ventures are underwritten differently. Experience, equipment, radius, cargo, and filings can all affect available options.

Do I need a DOT number to request a quote?

No. You can submit the form without it, but having your DOT number helps speed up review when available.

What information helps get a better quote?

Truck and trailer details, driver information, garaging address, loss runs, current declarations, cargo type, operating radius, and renewal date are all helpful.

Is this only for long haul truckers?

No. Long Haul Coverage can be positioned broadly for owner operators, hotshot, regional, flatbed, reefer, box truck, and fleet operations.
